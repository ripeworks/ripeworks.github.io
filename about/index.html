About

Located in Leelanau County, Michigan, Ripeworks provides development and design services for the web and portable devices.

Founder Mike Kruk

Mike has been developing and designing for agencies and technology shops since 2005. He began with a passion for programming that started at the young age of 8. Pair that with deep creative roots and a determination to get things done, and it's no wonder that Mike has worked with the likes of General Motors, Weight Watchers, and P&G to name a few.
