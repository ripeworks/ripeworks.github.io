About

Located in Leelanau County, Michigan, Ripeworks provides development services for the web and portable devices.

Founder

Mike Kruk

Mike has been developing and designing for agencies and technology shops since 2005. He began with a passion for programming that started at the young age of 8. Pair that with deep creative roots and a determination to get things done, it's no wonder that Mike has worked with the likes of General Motors, Yahoo, and the NFL to name a few.
